FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our security solutions and services.

Which is better, IP camera or analog camera?

IP cameras offer higher resolution (up to 4K), better image quality, remote access capabilities, and smart features like motion detection and analytics. Analog cameras are more budget-friendly and work well for basic surveillance needs. We recommend IP cameras for most modern installations, but analog systems can be suitable for smaller setups with limited budgets.

How many days of recording backup will I get?

Recording backup depends on the hard disk capacity, number of cameras, resolution, and recording settings. Typically, a 1TB hard disk provides 7-15 days of backup for 4 cameras at 2MP resolution. We recommend 2TB or higher for longer retention. We'll help you choose the right storage based on your specific needs during the site survey.

Do you provide remote support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 remote technical support for all our installations. Our team can remotely access your system (with your permission) to troubleshoot issues, configure settings, update firmware, and resolve most problems without an on-site visit. This ensures quick resolution and minimal downtime for your security system.

Do you serve outside Hyderabad?

Absolutely! While we're based in Hyderabad, we serve clients throughout Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. We've completed installations in cities like Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Warangal, Tirupati, and many other locations. For remote areas, we also provide comprehensive remote support services.

Do you provide warranty support?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranty. Hikvision and Dahua cameras typically have 2-year warranty, and NVR/DVR systems come with 1-2 years warranty. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for extended support and preventive maintenance beyond the warranty period.
